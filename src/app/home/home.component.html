Apply Online

A student can apply for the CET examination online where they can fill the details and pay the examination fees online as well.

Following information will need to be submitted

Category 1 - JCOs & OR (General/OBC) : Serving or Retired JCOs and OR of Army or equivalent in Air Force / Navy who do not belong to SC / ST category shall apply in this category.
2 : Serving or Retired JCOs and OR of Army or equivalent in Air Force / Navy who belong to SC category shall apply in this category.
3 : Serving or Retired JCOs and OR of Army or equivalent in Air Force / Navy who belong to ST category shall apply in this category.
4 : Sons of War Widows are eligible under this category.
5 : All wards of Civilians/Officers who do not belong to SC / ST category shall apply under this category.
6 : All wards of Civilians/Officers who belong to SC category shall apply under this category.
7 : All wards of Civilians/Officers who belong to ST category shall apply under this category.

* All disputes subject to Delhi jurisdiction.

Apply Offline

A student not looking to appliy for CET exam online, can download the colored application document and submit the filled document along with the DD of the examination fees.

Important instructions:

1 : Download the document after filling up the form .
2 : Verify your mobile number to complete the application download process. A form number will be generated for you to mention on the form.
3 : Take a coloured print out of the application form and fill in all the details including mentioning the form number provided in above step.
4 : Sons of War Widows are eligible under this category.
5 : All wards of Civilians/Officers who do not belong to SC / ST category shall apply under this category.
6 : All wards of Civilians/Officers who belong to SC category shall apply under this category.
7 : All wards of Civilians/Officers who belong to ST category shall apply under this category.

* All disputes subject to Delhi jurisdiction.
